Build out a Diner Menu using JavaScript

Build out a Diner menu in JAVASCRIPT. Here are your instructions to build that Diner: Bottega Diner.

Have the Main Menu and a Sides Menu.

You get one entree and two side choices at regular cost.

  • show them the entire menu (print out)

  • A user selects an entree. - “Waitress” makes a comment based on their selection - comment can either be a comparison of the two items, or
    - random comment pulled from a comment vault. - Tell them the price

  • repeat the above options for side choices (comment and a price) - “Waitress” makes a comment based on their selection - comment can either be a comparison of the two items, or
    - random comment pulled from a comment vault. - Tell them the price

  • total up the cost

BONUS Have breakfast, lunch, and dinner menu. Breakfast has different items, lunch and dinner have the same items but are different prices.

BONUS: Allow for item customization (how items are prepared, decide additional cost implications)
